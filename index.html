*Interests were shortened by the editor to contain only keywords.

**It takes the maximum rating of each skill among the selected people.

Controls:
  • Click on bars in the bar chart to select and unselect group members.
    (On the right it will show the interests and personal ratings of the last selected bar)
  • Click on radar chart legend to display or not display personal ratings or combined skills.
  • Hover over the bar or radar chart to get a list of the information displayed.
